Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chartered ONE a reputable company?

Chartered ONE is an online platform for legal and compliance services. Our team includes Chartered Accountants (CAs), Company Secretaries (CS), and lawyers who are committed to strict professional standards. We emphasize confidentiality and integrity in all our interactions, ensuring trustworthy and dependable support for our clients' business requirements.

How long has Chartered ONE been providing its services?

Chartered ONE began in 2018 headquartered in Hyderabad, India.

Is my data filed with Chartered ONE secure?

Chartered ONE uses 128-bit SSL encryption for the transmission of data and enables complete data privacy. Also, Chartered ONE is run by CA, CS & lawyers who as per their professional ethics are required to ensure full confidentiality of your data.

What are the GST services offered by Chartered ONE?

Chartered ONE provides Chartered GST filing software for accurate GST filing, reconciliation and claiming ITC. Chartered GST also facilitates the generation of e-invoices and e-way bills.

How long does it take to form a Company with Chartered ONE?

It takes approximately 7 days to form a company with Chartered ONE. However, the days may vary depending on your company type, name, ready to file documents.

Does Chartered ONE provide import-export services?

Import-export services provided by Chartered One are Certificate Of Origin, IEC Registration, IEC Renewal, Freight Forwarding Services, APTA Certificate Registration, SAPTA Registration, SAFTA License, GSP Registration, Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) and many more.

Does Chartered ONE have its own dashboard?

Yes, Chartered ONE Dashboard allows you to easily select services, apply quickly, and communicate directly with your assigned Chartered Accountants (CAs), Company Secretaries (CS), and lawyers. You can conveniently maintain your secretarial documents, receive certificates promptly, and access GST, income tax, and TDS software as needed.

Is Chartered ONE Dashboard free?

Yes, Chartered ONE Dashboard is completely free, you just need to login using your gmail account or mobile number to access the Dashboard.
