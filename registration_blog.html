Procedure for Private Limited Company Registration in India

The process of registering a Private Limited Company in India involves the following steps:

As per guidelines, all applicatons to the Registrar of Companies are filed in digital format and are therefore required to be authentcated using a digital signature of the authorized signatory. Proposed directors must acquire a Class 2 or Class 3 DSC for digital signing of incorporaton documents. Chartered ONE offers Digital Signature service, fulfilling this requirement of obtaining a DSC for ROC fillings.

The next step is that you'll need to submit a SPICe RUN form, which is part of the larger SPICe+ form. Chartered ONE ensures you secure your dream company name during an inital availability check. However, if your preferred name is already registered, we provide unlimited alternatve company name searches to help you find the perfect fit.

Applicaton for Incorporaton is draed using SPICe+ form, a simplified proforma for incorporatng the company electronically, along with SPICe e-MOA & e-AOA. Chartered ONE makes your company incorporaton easy and simple.

Upon successful verificaton by the ROC, Private Limited Co will receive a Certficate of Incorporaton, a PAN certficate, & a TAN certficate

Upon obtaining PAN, Company will be eligible to open a current bank account. Chartered ONE offers multple banks to choose from, with the process being entrely remote & streamlining the experience for you.

ESI registraton is required when the employee count is 9 and EPF registraton is required when the employee count is 20. Voluntary registraton opton is also available even if below threshold limit. Professional Tax is applicable in some states based on the earnings of the employee. Ensure a smooth ESI, EPF, PT registraton for your Company with Chartered ONE's expert guidance.

IEC registraton for Private Limited Company is easy, Chartered ONE streamlines the process for you. A PLC in India is required to obtain an Import Export Code (IEC) if it imports or exports goods. The IEC is a 10-digit number issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) that is required for all import and export transactons. The IEC is valid for life once it is issued.

GST registraton is required only if the turnover of private limited company exceeds 20 lakhs, then they need to get GST Registraton. Upon registraton, they get many benefits like claiming tax credits on purchases, increased credibility, reduced cost of purchases, Loan approval at lower rates, etc. Chartered ONE ensures a seamless GST registraton process & tmely filing of GST returns with accuracy.

Chartered ONE guarantees integrity, accuracy, and proper accountng & Book Keeping.

Chartered ONE handles all applicable tax and social security contributons, including TDS, EPF, and ESI. Our secure and efficient filing process ensures tmely submissions, complete accuracy, and minimizes the risk of penaltes

Upon Incorporaton, a Private Limited Company needs to have all secretarial compliances, to ensure that the company operates efficiently and transparently. Chartered ONE enables you to have a dedicated team of CS to comply with the rules & regulatons of Company law.

A competent auditor must conduct an annual audit of the companys financial statements. Chartered One's team of experienced Chartered Accountants ensures a smooth and reliable statutory audit for your company.

Tax audits are compulsory if a business's gross turnover surpasses 1 crore. With Chartered One's team of Chartered Accountants, tax audits are handled smoothly, saving you tme and hassle.

As per Company Law, all listed companies and unlisted public companies with a turnover of 200 Crores or more must regularly perform internal audits. However, all companies, regardless of size or industry, benefit from gaining valuable insights into operatonal efficiency, accountng methods & Corporate Governance through regular internal audits. Empower your business for success with Chartered ONE internal audit expertse.

An auditor must be appointed within 15 days of the AGM to audit the company's financial statements annually for a maximum of five financial years. The company must inform the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) of the appointment using Form ADT-1. Chartered ONE takes the worry out of secretarial compliance. With a dedicated CS professional by your side, you can focus on running your business.

Annual Returns have to be filed Must be filed with the Registrar of Companies (RoC) in Form MGT-7

Annual Financial Statements along with Auditors report will have to be filed in AOC-4 within 6 months from the date of the closing of the Financial Year. FS must be audited by a practcing Chartered Accountant. Chartered ONE not only ensures you file your Annual Financial Statement on tme but also gets your Books audited by CA.

Income Tax Return is required to be filed in Form ITR-7, within 60 days from the end of the financial year i.e. the form should be filed by 30th May. However, the corporaton may be exempt if registered under Sectons 12A and 80G. Chartered ONE ensures you file your return in tme & hassle-free.
